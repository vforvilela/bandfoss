# JBL BandBox Solo vs JBL BandBox Trio — Differences

A side-by-side comparison based on the official JBL Owner's Manuals (Solo V7, 2026; Trio V5, 2025).

## Positioning / Form Factor

| Aspect | BandBox Solo | BandBox Trio |
|---|---|---|
| Tagline | Compact AI-powered practice amp and speaker | Portable AI-powered practice amp and speaker |
| Form factor | Pocket / handheld speaker with wrist strap | Portable amp/PA with carrying handle |
| Net weight | 0.53 kg (1.17 lbs) | 6.65 kg (14.66 lbs) |
| Dimensions (W × H × D) | 150 × 87 × 55 mm | 344 × 265 × 228 mm |

## In the Box

| Solo | Trio |
|---|---|
| Main unit + Quick Start / Safety / Warranty docs | Main unit + **Power cord** + docs |

## Audio Hardware

| Aspect | Solo | Trio |
|---|---|---|
| Transducers | 1 × 2.25" full range | 1 × 6.5" woofer **+ 2 × 25 mm silk dome tweeters** |
| Output power | 18 W RMS | **135 W RMS** |
| Frequency response | 73 Hz – 20 kHz | **50 Hz – 20 kHz** |
| Headphone output | Max 1 Vrms (16/32 Ω) | Max 1 Vrms (16/32 Ω) |
| Lineout | — | Max 1 Vrms, 2-channel (Aux-in passthrough) |

## Power

| Aspect | Solo | Trio |
|---|---|---|
| Power input | 5 V / 3 A (max) via USB-C | AC 100–240 V (50/60 Hz) **or** 5/12/15/20 V DC, 3 A max via USB-C PD |
| Battery | Built-in Li-ion 17 Wh (3.6 V / 4722 mAh) | Li-ion 68 Wh (7.2 V / 9444 mAh) — **user-replaceable JBL BATTERY 400** |
| Charge time | 3.5 h (speaker off) | Not specified (uses included AC cord or 60 W PD USB-C) |
| Music play time | Up to 6 h | Up to **10 h** |
| Charges other devices | No | **Yes** — USB-C charge-out for mobile devices |
| Auto-off | After 20 min of inactivity (no input plugged) | After 20 min standby; press power to resume |

## Inputs

| Input | Solo | Trio |
|---|---|---|
| Channels | 1 combined input | **4 channels** (CH1–CH4) on a mixer |
| Instrument / Mic input | 1 × 6.35 mm (1/4") TRS — single shared port | **CH1**: 1/4" or **XLR/TRS combo**, mic/Hi-Z guitar slider · **CH2**: 1/4" or XLR/TRS combo, mic/line slider · **CH3**: dedicated 6.35 mm Hi-Z guitar |
| 3.5 mm AUX in | — | **CH4** AUX (3.5 mm) |
| Bluetooth | Yes (CH "Music") | Yes (CH4, shared with AUX/USB-C) |
| USB-C audio in | Yes | Yes (CH4) |
| Built-in microphone | **Yes** (works with headphones in singer/singing-guitarist modes) | **No built-in mic** |

## Outputs / Routing

| Output | Solo | Trio |
|---|---|---|
| Headphone | 3.5 mm TRS | 3.5 mm TRS |
| Line/PA out | Routed via headphone jack (use Y-splitter or DI) | Dedicated **PASSTHRU** 6.35 mm TS output |
| Connect to PartyBox | — | **Yes** — passthru to JBL PartyBox guitar input |

## Controls / UI

| Aspect | Solo | Trio |
|---|---|---|
| Knobs | 1 control knob | 1 main knob + **5 functional knobs** |
| Display | Small dot-matrix segment display | **Larger graphical display** |
| Dedicated buttons | Power, Bluetooth, Menu, STEM AI | Power, MIXER, CH1–CH4 channel buttons, STEM AI, Bluetooth |
| Channel mixing | Implicit (guitar / mic / music) | Full **mixer screen** with per-channel volume + clipping LED |
| Display flip orientation | — | **Yes** (Display Flip option) |
| Language menu | — | **Yes** (Language selection) |

## Musician Modes

| Solo | Trio |
|---|---|
| Three preset musician types: **Singing Guitarist**, **Guitarist**, **Singer** (auto-configures controls) | No musician-type modes — channels are individually configured on the mixer |

| Metronome | Yes (30–240 BPM) | Yes (30–240 BPM) |

| **Drum machine** | — | **Yes** (multiple drum styles) |
| Guitar presets | Yes (on-product effects, switch via menu) | Yes — **editable 8-module effect chain** with amp/cab/mod/delay/reverb/etc. |
| Pitch shift | — | Yes (functional knob 5 in stem screen) |
| Karaoke mode | In JBL ONE app | **On-product** (Track Settings page, low-latency 500 ms) |

## STEM AI (real-time audio source separation)

| Aspect | Solo | Trio |
|---|---|---|
| Stems | Guitar / Vocal / Others | Vocal / Others (default) — configurable Guitar/Vocal/Others/Drums via app |
| Modes | Single mode | **Multi-stem separation** (2 s latency) **and Karaoke** (500 ms latency) |
| Mute / unmute | Press knob | Per-track on Track Settings page |
| Invert (mute everything except guitar) | Hold STEM AI button | Via Track Settings |

## Headline Differentiators

- **Solo** is a single-input, ultra-portable battery practice tool with a built-in mic, a wrist strap, and three "musician type" auto-config modes — designed to grab and go.
- **Trio** is a 4-channel mixing practice amp/PA with XLR/TRS combo jacks, a real mixer UI, ~7.5× the wattage, dual tweeters + 6.5" woofer, AC power, a swappable battery, an editable 8-module guitar effect chain, an on-board drum machine, on-product karaoke mode, and PartyBox passthrough — designed for full band/stage practice.
